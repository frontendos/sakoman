Welcom to Mars

As I stand out here in the wonders of the unknown at Hadley, I sort of realize there’s a fundamental truth to our nature, Man must explore . . . and this is exploration at its greatest.Many say exploration is part of our destiny, but it’s actually our duty to future generations and their quest to ensure the survival of the human species. Buy why, some say, the moon? Why choose this as our goal? And they may as well ask why climb the highest mountain? For those who have seen the Earth from space, and for the hundreds and perhaps thousands more who will, the experience most certainly changes your perspective. The things that we share in our world are far more valuable than those which divide us./p>
